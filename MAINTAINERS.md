## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing and merging pull requests
- Triaging issues and feature requests
- Making architectural decisions
- Managing releases and versioning
- Maintaining documentation
- Ensuring code quality and test coverage

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who demonstrate sustained commitment to the project through quality contributions may be considered for maintainer status. Criteria include:

- Consistent high-quality pull requests
- Active participation in issue discussions
- Understanding of project architecture and goals
- Adherence to project coding standards
